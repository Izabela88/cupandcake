a few words about us

Our bakery bakes cupcakes to order and delivers them to customers' homes. We started in 2009 with a team of two. Currently we are hiring over 100 employees and we send our cupcakes all over the Great Britain. We only use organic products to prepare our muffins and select products from local suppliers. Our secret is passion and love for sweets. We are happy that you want to become part of our sweet family and try our delicious products! Enjoy!
